Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

1. Information We Collect

Our applications are built with user privacy and security as our top priority. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information (PII), telemetry data, or usage analytics to external servers. Any application configuration, keys, or preferences you generate remain strictly on your local device.

2. How We Use the Information

Because we do not collect or harvest any user data, no personal information is processed, analyzed, or utilized by our team. Any processing of data happens purely dynamically on-device to execute the core functions of the application layout and interface features.

3. Whether the Data is Shared

We do not sell, trade, rent, or otherwise share any user data with third-party organizations, advertising networks, or external analytics firms. Your data never leaves your sandboxed device environment.

4. Data Security

Any application data configuration, security values, or personal preferences are kept isolated within the secure iOS user sandbox environment. Secure data parameters utilize native iOS hardware-level Keychain Services for system encryption storage, ensuring robust protection against unauthorized local access.

5. User Rights and Data Deletion

We fully support your right to control your data. Because all application parameters are stored strictly on-device, you have absolute control. You can delete all application data, preferences, or saved files at any time simply by uninstalling the application from your iOS device. No residual profiles or cloud backups are maintained by us.

6. Contact Information

If you have any questions, feedback, or operational queries regarding our privacy practices or application protocols, please feel free to reach out to us via official campus channels at the Apple Developers Group, MIT Manipal.
